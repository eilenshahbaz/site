Here's a timeline of Brady's life

  • 1977 - Born in San Mateo, California.
  • 1982 - At the age of four, witnesses "The Catch" at Candlestick Park, San Francisco, CA.
  • 1995 - Graduates from Junipero Serra High School in San Mateo. Brady considers attending Cal. Ultimately chooses to atttend University of Michigan as the seventh quarteback on the depth chart.
  • 1998 - Earns starting position as quarterback, leads the Wolverines to a Big Ten Conference title and caps the season with a victory in the Citrus Bowl.
  • 1999 - Labeled with the moniker, the "Comeback Kid", Brady leads the Wolverines to a plethora of comebacks, capping his final college football career with back to back comebacks against Ohio State and Alabama.
  • 2000 - In the 6th round of the 2000 NFL Draft, the New England Patriots draft, at the 199th pick, Tom Brady, Michigan.
  • 2002 - Takes over the starting role after an injury to Drew Bledsoe and leads the New England Patriots to a victory over the Saint Louis "The Greatest Show on Turf" Rams in Super Bowl XXVI (2001 postseason).
  • 2004 - Leads the New England Patriots to a Super Bowl victory over the Carolina Panthers (2003 postseason).
  • 2005 - Defends world title with a third Super Bowl victory in four years, this time besting the Philadelpia Eagles (2004 postseason).
